Parameters of the model

Number of individuals (\(N\))
Probability to join shelter 1 (\(\mu_1\))
Probability to join shelter 2 (\(\mu_2\))
Individual probability to leave shelter 1 (\(\theta_1\))
Individual probability to leave shelter 2 (\(\theta_2\))
Interattraction in shelter 1 (\(\alpha_1\))
Interattraction in shelter 2 (\(\alpha_2\))
Threshold value (\(K\))
Cooperativity (\(m\))

Parameters of the simulation

Number of timesteps
Number of simulations


Binary choice cockroach model



$$\frac{d X_i}{dt} = \mu _i\left(N - \sum_{j=1}^2 X_j\right) -\frac{\theta_i}{K^m+\left(\alpha_i X_i\right)^m}X_i \quad i=1,2$$
